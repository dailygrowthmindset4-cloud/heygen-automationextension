Frequently Asked Questions

Extension is not working

Refresh the HeyGen page and ensure you are logged into your account.

Automation buttons are not appearing

Verify that the extension is enabled in your browser and that you are using a supported version of Chrome.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email with a description of the issue, screenshots if available, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Feature requests and improvement suggestions are welcome.

Response Time
